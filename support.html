Common Issues and Solutions

1. I see an ad that doesn’t close or is inappropriate. What should I do?

Please report the issue to us by providing the ad details (if possible) and a screenshot at fire.mountain.dev@hotmail.com.

2. My in-app purchase didn’t process. What should I do?

All in-app purchases on iOS are managed directly by Apple. If your purchase was not processed, please:

  • Check your purchase history in your Apple ID account.
  • Ensure the transaction was completed successfully.
  • Contact Apple Support for assistance: Apple Support - Billing & Subscriptions.

3. The app crashes or doesn’t load. What can I do?

Try the following steps:

  • Restart your device.
  • Ensure the app is updated to the latest version.
  • Clear the app’s cache (on Android).
  • If the issue persists, contact us with details about your device (model, operating system version).
